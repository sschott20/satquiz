Question
When they were first introduced to western Europe from Byzantium in the eleventh century, table forks were met with much resistance. The
Bishop of Ostia, St. Peter Damian, condemned the eating utensils because he considered ______ dangerous and unnecessary luxury items. 
Which choice completes the text so that it conforms to the conventions of Standard English?
Answer
A. them
B. this
C. that
D. it
Correct Answer: A
Rationale
Choice A is the best answer. The convention being tested is pronoun-antecedent agreement. The plural pronoun "them" agrees in number with
the plural antecedent "utensils.
